For You

Hi, Jay! I am so happy that you are reading this right now, it's your birthday! Happy birthday, twin! I decided to make you this because I thought it would be a little token of appreciation to your persona. I am not too good with words, but I want to try my best for you. Today is a really special day because it’s the day someone who means a lot to me came into the world. Even if we’re not next to each other in real life, you’ve still become such an important part of mine. I don’t think I say it enough, but I’m really grateful for you. For your presence, your time, and for all the little moments we’ve shared online that somehow ended up meaning way more than “just talking.” You make ordinary days feel lighter just by being there. I hope this year brings you things that make you feel proud of yourself, moments that make you laugh until your stomach hurts, and people who treat you with the kindness you deserve so much. You deserve good things, even on the days you forget that you do. I know life isn’t always easy, but I hope you always remember you’re not alone in it. I’m really glad I got to meet you, and i dont regret any second of it. So today, I just want you to feel celebrated, appreciated, and genuinely loved. You’re special, Jay. More than you probably realize. Happy Birthday again! I hope with all my heart we get more memories, more laughs, and more years of being together, even from a distance. — tea<33
